Worn with meaning

Handmade Bead Jewellery rooted in
Ukrainian tradition

Oberih — meaning "protective charm" in Ukrainian — brings centuries-old ornamental patterns into contemporary wear. Each piece is handcrafted using traditional bead weaving techniques, with geometric patterns inspired by Ukrainian folk art.

Bold colors, meticulous detail, and hours of skilled labor transform glass beads into wearable cultural artifacts. This is jewelry for those who wear their story.
