Autumn Sylianka Statement Necklace

€49

A vibrant statement necklace featuring traditional Ukrainian Sylianka patterns in rich autumn tones. Deep reds, golden oranges, and forest greens. Hand-beaded using traditional techniques, this piece takes approximately 24-26 hours to create. The complex geometric structure and bold color palette make this necklace perfect for those who want maximum visual impact. Materials: Glass beads, brass findings, cotton thread. Clasp: Brass toggle with extension chain.

Pattern Meaning

The circular medallion patterns represent the sun and life force, with rays symbolizing energy spreading outward. Red signifies vitality, gold represents prosperity, and green symbolizes growth—a celebration of harvest and abundance in Ukrainian tradition.

Made-to-Order

Each piece is handcrafted to order. Production time is approximately 2-3 weeks from order confirmation. You'll receive progress photos as your piece is created, and we'll notify you when it's ready to ship.
