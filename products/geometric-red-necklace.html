Geometric Red Statement Necklace

€55

A bold statement necklace featuring traditional Ukrainian geometric patterns in high contrast black, red, and white. Hand-beaded using traditional techniques, this piece takes approximately 26-28 hours to create. The strong geometric structure and vibrant colors make this necklace perfect for those who view accessories as self expression. Materials: Glass beads, brass findings, cotton thread. Clasp: Brass toggle with extension chain.

Pattern Meaning

This geometric pattern draws from traditional Ukrainian cross-stitch motifs symbolizing protection and strength. The interlocking diamonds represent unity and continuity, while the bold red color signifies vitality and life force in Ukrainian folk tradition.

Made-to-Order

Each piece is handcrafted to order. Production time is approximately 2-3 weeks from order confirmation. You'll receive progress photos as your piece is created, and we'll notify you when it's ready to ship.
