Geometric Red Necklace

€80

A bold statement necklace featuring traditional Ukrainian geometric patterns in high-contrast black, red, and white. Hand-beaded using traditional techniques, this piece takes approximately 18–20 hours to create. The strong geometric structure and vibrant colors make this necklace perfect for those who view accessories as self-expression.

Pattern Meaning

This geometric pattern draws from traditional Ukrainian cross-stitch motifs symbolizing protection and strength. The interlocking diamonds represent unity and continuity, while the bold red color signifies vitality and life force in Ukrainian folk tradition.

Made-to-Order

Each piece is handcrafted to order. Production time is approximately 2–3 weeks from order confirmation. You'll receive progress photos as your piece is created, and we'll notify you when it's ready to ship.
