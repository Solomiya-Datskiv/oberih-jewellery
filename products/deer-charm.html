Little Deer Charm

€42

A sweet three-dimensional beaded deer in golden tones with brown antlers. Hand-beaded using traditional techniques, this piece takes approximately 6 - 8 hours to create. The careful color gradient and dimensional construction make this charm perfect for those seeking unique handmade accessories. Materials: Glass beads, cotton thread. Attachment: Metal loop for keychain or chain.

Pattern Meaning

Deer symbolize gentleness, grace, and connection to nature in Ukrainian tradition. This charm brings calm and natural beauty to everyday life.
